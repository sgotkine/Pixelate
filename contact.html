FAQ's

It's normal to have questions. Don't worry because we have collated some frequently asked questions and given you the answers.

What are NFTs?

An NFT, or Non-Fungible Token, is a unique digital asset that represents ownership or proof of authenticity of a specific item or piece of content, such as digital art, music, videos, or collectibles, on the blockchain.

What services does Pixelate offer to galleries and museums?

Pixelate offers digitisation services for galleries and museums, helping them showcase their collections in the digital realm. We provide expertise in NFTs and cutting-edge technology to maximize reach and profitability.

How can Pixelate help my gallery or museum reach a wider audience?

Pixelate collaborates closely with galleries and museums to digitise their collections and make them accessible to a global audience through our platform.

How does Pixelate ensure the security and authenticity of digital art?

Pixelate utilises blockchain technology to ensure the security and authenticity of digital art assets. Each artwork is tokenised as an NFT, providing proof of ownership and authenticity on the blockchain.

How can I get started with Pixelate's services for my gallery or museum?

Getting started with Pixelate is easy. Simply reach out to us through our contact page to schedule a meeting. Our team will work with you to discuss your needs and develop a solution for digitising your art collection.

Can Pixelate help my gallery or museum monetise its digital assets?

Pixelate can help your gallery or museum monetise its digital assets through the sale of NFTs. By tokenising your artwork as NFTs and offering them for sale on our platform, you can generate revenue and increase the value of your collection.
