How it Works

Understand the process of Pixelate





Unique NFTs from every painting

At Pixelate, we empower artists and galleries to tokenize their artwork as non-fungible tokens (NFTs), unlocking new opportunities for ownership and investment.

Here's how it works:


Individual NFTs for each painting

From each painting, we can create multiple NFTs, each representing a unique digital asset available for purchase separately. This allows collectors to own a digital version of the artwork while the original piece remains safely preserved in the gallery.


Fractional ownership with Pixelate

Alternatively, we offer the option to split the artwork into squares, allowing investors to own a fractional piece of the painting. Each square corresponds to specific coordinates within the artwork, providing a unique and tangible ownership experience.


Preservation of original artwork

Rest assured, the original piece of art remains untouched and securely displayed in the gallery, preserving its integrity and value. Our digitisation process ensures that the artwork is safeguarded while providing new avenues for ownership and appreciation.
